My data

My data is a project with mission of return you a control to your personal data.

If you ever had a problem that you don't know who use your data, how to update or delete them from different places this is a place for you.

For example, your phone number, email or home address is used in huge amount of places. Do you remember all of them? ;)

My data is one of a Web Tree project. For now My data is on early development stage. We can't give any guaranties.
